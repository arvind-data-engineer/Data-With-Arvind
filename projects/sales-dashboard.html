Challenge

Leaders need consolidated revenue, order, and growth metrics across regions and product lines, without manual spreadsheet assembly.

Solution

A dashboard focused on business-critical sales indicators

Revenue summary

Shows total revenue, month-over-month growth, and category contributions.

Order health

Tracks order volume, average order value, and fulfillment performance.

Regional performance

Compares sales performance across geographic regions and business units.
